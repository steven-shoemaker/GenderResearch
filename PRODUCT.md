# Gender Research

## Register

Product (personal research tool UI).

## Users

One primary user: a researcher analyzing job descriptions for gendered language patterns. Desktop-first, occasional mobile reference.

## Product purpose

Paste job description text, compare against a customizable masculine/feminine word list, see percentages and in-text highlights, and save entries with metadata and optional PDF snapshots. Cloud-backed, single-user, no auth.

## Emotional job

Feel capable and systematic: serious personal research, not a toy or enterprise HR product.

## Social job

Feel like a personal lab notebook: trustworthy, calm, private.

## Anti-references

- Corporate HR SaaS (navy dashboards, hero metrics, card grids of features)
- Stereotype cartoon gender colors (hot pink, boys-club blue)
- Generic violet “AI startup” chrome
- Alarm-red error theater for non-critical states

## Strategic principles

- One obvious primary action per screen zone
- Paste and analyze before save; lexicon save is explicit
- Vocabulary: Entries, Analyze, Save entry, Recompute entry, Word list
- Long JD text must be comfortable to read and scroll
